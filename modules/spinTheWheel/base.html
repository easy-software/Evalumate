Strive for 5 Challenge

The Strive-for-5 Challenge promotes self-awareness, which is required for developing our emotional maturity. Being the 5 is having a willingness to grow, the courage to be vulnerable with others, and being self-reflective.

As research suggests, it takes 21 days to form a new habit. The first 3 weeks serve as practice for personal growth. The final week will put your emotional development to the test.

This challenge is about stepping outside of your comfort zone. We have purposely created some tasks to sound ambiguous, as an opportunity for you to interpret them in a way that resonates most profoundly with you.
