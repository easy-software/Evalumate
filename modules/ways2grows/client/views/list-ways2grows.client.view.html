Strive-for-5 Challenge


The Strive-for-5 Challenge promotes our self-awareness, which is required for developing great emotional maturity.

What does Strive-for-5 mean?


Being a “5” refers to a level of “great maturity” from the Emotional Maturity Scale, discussed in the LovEd Clarity program. However, the “Strive-for-5” Challenge is for everyone, even if you yet to experience Clarity. This challenge is about stepping outside of your comfort zone. We have purposely created some tasks to sound ambiguous, as an opportunity for you to interpret them in a way that resonates most profoundly with you.Because research suggests it takes 21 days to form a new habit, the first 3 weeks of this challenge will serve as practice for our personal growth. To really solidify our emotional development, the final week will put us to the test.
